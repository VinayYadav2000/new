ABOUT US

Since 1998, Ajanta Universal Fabrics Limited has been thriving to bring maximum excellence in its products. AUFL is well aware of the expectations of the international customers and has realised the place of quality deeply.

Greige cotton fabric is used extensively in various industrial applications like Awnings, Banners, Construction, Filtration, Flags, Military, Packaging, Safety & Protective, Tents, Abrasives etc. AUFL's Greige cotton fabric is used for all these applications. AUFL's Greige fabrics are being exported. It has a simultaneously presence in the domestic market.
